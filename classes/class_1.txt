class Restaurant

Attributes:
open (Boolean)
number_of_reservations (Integer)
dinner_menu (Array)
server_name (String)

Methods:
is_open (uses open and if restaurant is open, then is_open returns true and if restaurant is closed, is_open returns false)
add_reservations(uses number_of_reservations, increases the amount of reservations and reassigns new amount to number_of_reservations)
choose_dish (uses dinner_menu and picks one dish from a list of dinner dishes)
prints_server_name (uses server_name and prints the server's name in all caps)
